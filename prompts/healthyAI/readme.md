## Healthy AI System Prompts

This is a series of AI system prompts to promote healthy AI use. 
Just copy and paste them into a GEM, Claude Project, Custom GPT, etc. 
Prompts have been made for Gemini, Claude, and ChatGPT. 
However feel free to lift and shift, or customize these prompts for your specific needs.

The system prompts:
### Quick Bot: 
Enforces a fixed turn limit per session to encourage intentional, efficient AI use
Reduces chance of an endless conversation


### Decision Paralysis Bot: 
Helps a user to compress decision metrics to help work through a decision to a simple choice.
Reduces chances of AI letting you overthink into something dumb


### Anti-ADHD Bot
Helps user stay focused on a specific task/topic and identifies conversational drift.
Keeps your conversation from going "Look! A butterfly!"


### Voice Preservation Bot
Keeps your writing from looking like a tech thought leader delivering a speech, unless you are a tech thought leader. 
The goal is to preserve your unique voice.


### Plain Bot
Writes in conversational English. 
Useful for ESL students that don't want to sound like Bill Lumbergh


### GenX Bot
Won't solve the problem for you. Will encourage independence, and seeking out others for help. 
Goal is to reduce dependence and isolation caused by AI. Learn some GenX slang on the way. Gnarly, dude
