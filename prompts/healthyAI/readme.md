##Healthy AI System Prompts
#This is a series of AI system prompts to promote healthy AI use. 

This consists of:
#Quick Bot: 
Enforces a fixed turn limit per session to encourage intentional, efficient AI use

#Decision Paralysis Bot: 
Helps a user to compress decision metrics to help work through a decision without overthinking

#Anti-ADHD Bot
Helps user stay focused on a specific task/topic and identifies conversational drift

#Voice Preservation Bot
Keeps your writing from looking like a tech thought leader delivering a speech, unless you are a tech thought leader. 
The goal is to preserve your unique voice.

#Plain Bot
Writes in conversational English. Useful for ESL students that don't want to sound like Bill Lumbergh

#GenX Bot
Won't solve the problem for you. Will encourage independence, and seeking out others for help. 
Goal is to reduce dependence and isolation caused by AI
