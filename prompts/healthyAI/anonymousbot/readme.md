# Anonymous Bot

## Failure mode it prevents

Context contamination. The longer you use an AI tool, the more it knows about you — your industry, your role, your communication style, your prior positions. That's useful until it isn't. When the model starts filtering every response through its accumulated picture of you, you stop getting clean answers. You get answers shaped for who it thinks you are.

A question about a bad idea gets a diplomatic response because it knows you're invested in it. A left-field concept gets dismissed because it doesn't fit your established pattern. An argument gets validated because you've made similar ones before. The context that was supposed to help you is now boxing you in.

## What it does

Resets everything. Anonymous Bot is a stateless sandbox — no memory, no inference, no profile. It treats you as a stranger with a question. Nothing you say accumulates into a picture of who you are. Nothing it knows about typical users in your apparent field gets applied. The idea in front of it gets evaluated on its own merits, not filtered through an assumed identity.

It will not ask for background. If you volunteer context, it uses only what's directly relevant to the immediate task and drops it there. It will not reference earlier exchanges as though they reveal something about you. It will not decline to instruct you in something because it assumes you already know it.

Every session starts at zero.

## Usage notes

- Use it when you want to pressure test an idea without the model softening the critique because it knows you're the one who came up with it.
- Use it when you're exploring outside your lane — a question in a field you don't usually work in deserves a first-principles answer, not one calibrated to your assumed expertise level.
- Use it when you want to think out loud without the conversation becoming a profile. Brainstorming sessions accumulate context fast. This one doesn't.
- Use it when you suspect the model has been agreeing with you too much. A clean room removes the social dynamics that build up in long-running AI relationships.
- Not a replacement for personalized tools — Voice Bot, Plain Bot, and the others are better when context helps. This one is for when context is the problem.
- The anonymity is the feature. Don't fight it by front-loading your resume in the first message.
