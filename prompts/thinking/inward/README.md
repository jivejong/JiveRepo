# Inward

Tools that multiply perspective onto the user themselves — voice, belief, contradiction, unexamined pattern — rather than onto something external they've brought. Every tool here reflects a pattern back as a hypothesis to confirm or correct, never as a finished verdict about who the user is. None of these are therapy; each one has a guardrail instructing it to step out of its format and respond directly if real distress surfaces.

## Voice Finder Bot

**File**: `voice-finder-bot-system-prompt.md`

Helps the user discover and sharpen a voice that's already theirs — writing voice (tone, rhythm, word choice) or reasoning voice (what they notice first, what they're allergic to) — never by writing the finished sentence for them. Two modes, chosen upfront. Writing Voice mode runs constraint extraction (what you cringe at) → sample reflection (patterns in real samples) → differential contrast (deliberately wrong rewrites to react against), used selectively. Reasoning Voice mode runs allergy mapping (what arguments you distrust even when valid) → trace-back (what you noticed first in a real decision) → cross-domain check (does the same fingerprint show up in an unrelated problem).

## Cognitive Archaeology

**File**: `cognitive-archaeology-bot-system-prompt.md`

Excavates how the user actually thinks, from real artifacts (writing, decisions, analogies) rather than self-report. Two modes, chosen upfront:

- **Personal Epistemology Explorer** — what the user actually trusts when establishing certainty (evidence, authority, experience, consensus, intuition, models), discovered from real instances rather than declared values.
- **Intellectual Lineage Discovery** — traces a specific belief or instinct back toward a plausible origin (family, profession, culture, education), held explicitly as speculative tracing rather than established fact.

## Metaphor Excavation

**File**: `metaphor-excavation-bot-system-prompt.md`

Surfaces the recurring figurative frame someone reaches for without noticing (life as journey, battle, system, garden, game) from how they actually talk about something, then goes a step further than most reflection tools: runs an actual swap, redescribing the same situation through a genuinely different metaphor's vocabulary and logic, so the user can see what becomes visible or invisible depending on the frame in use.

## Curiosity Cartography

**File**: `curiosity-cartography-bot-system-prompt.md`

Maps what repeatedly pulls the user's attention without being resolved — curiosity, not expertise. Gathers scattered, uncurated interests, looks for recurring pulls and unexpected connectors across domains that don't obviously relate, and tries to name the shape of the pull itself rather than just listing the subjects it attaches to. Leaves unresolved questions unresolved; doesn't prescribe what to do with the map.

## Contradiction Mapping

**File**: `contradiction-mapping-bot-system-prompt.md` · checklist included

Surfaces a genuine contradiction in the user's values or behavior and holds it open rather than resolving it — the explicit opposite instinct from most advice and most self-help. Both sides of the contradiction get stated with equal weight; the central guardrail is resisting any synthesis that quietly merges the two into one resolved preference. Entry can be user-named or bot-discovered from open-ended material.

## Epistemic Tracing

**File**: `epistemic-tracing-bot-system-prompt.md`

Audits a stated belief by repeatedly asking what specific underlying data point supports it and how that data point was obtained — walking down the ladder of inference until it hits either real primary evidence or an exposed, unsupported assumption. The mechanic requires genuine iteration (several rounds of "and how do you know that"); a single question-and-answer exchange doesn't count as a trace.

## Cognitive Bias Mapper

**File**: `cognitive-bias-mapper-bot-system-prompt.md`

Scans a piece of the user's own raw, emotionally-invested writing (a rant, a defensive justification) for linguistic markers of known cognitive biases — absolutist language, selective evidence, attribution asymmetry, sunk-cost framing — and maps where specific phrases are doing that work. Treats findings as features of a passage written under pressure, not as a character assessment; calibrated against over-flagging, since finding "bias" in every sentence defeats the tool's credibility.

## Values Through Tradeoffs

**File**: `values-through-tradeoffs-bot-system-prompt.md`

Infers what the user actually values from a real history of tradeoffs they've made, rather than from what they'd say they value. Stated values are treated as claims to test, not findings to record; the real deliverable is the pattern across several real, specific past tradeoffs, held precisely rather than rounded up into a virtue-word label faster than the evidence supports.
